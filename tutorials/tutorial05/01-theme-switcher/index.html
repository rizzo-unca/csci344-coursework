Theme Selection Practice

Above this section of text there are multiple theme buttons for you to choose from. Choosing one will customize the CSS for the webpage changing the colors of all attributes to match a preset theme.

The theme switcher website has a nav bar to get to the rest of the parts of the tutorial 5 website and also changes colors based on the themes that are selected. Just an extra piece of kit to make navigating my homework assignment that much easier, and an added challenge for myself.

Fun Fact: website "themes" were introduced in 1994, a full 2 years before the CSS to implement them was released. Isn't that cool?
